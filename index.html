About Me

Hello! My name is Jack and I am an aspiring developer. After high scool, I attended a year of community college with the intention of persuing Information Technology. As it happens to many, life got in the way and plans changed. After having several successful colleagues to guide me back into the field, I am currently working to complete the MERN Full Stack program with the University of Minnesota. Born and raised in the northern suburbs of the Twin Cities, I currently live in Shoreview with my wife. In my spare time, I enjoy hiking outdoors, gaming and reading.
